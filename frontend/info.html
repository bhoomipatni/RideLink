How RideLink Works

RideLink makes campus travel simple, affordable, and secure by connecting verified students who are heading in the same direction.

Step 1: Verify Your Student Account

Sign up using your university email to ensure all users are verified members of your campus community.

Create a Profile

Add your basic information and travel preferences.

Confirm Campus Email

Verification ensures a safe, student-only rideshare network.

Step 2: Post or Find a Ride

Whether you're driving or looking for a ride, RideLink helps you connect with other students traveling to the same destination.

Post a Trip

Enter your destination, departure time, and available seats.

Search for Rides

Browse available trips that match your schedule and location.

Step 3: Split Costs Seamlessly

Easily divide gas costs using integrated payment options.

Secure Payments

Pay or request payments directly through Venmo or PayPal.

Transparent Cost Sharing

See exactly how much you owe before confirming your ride.
